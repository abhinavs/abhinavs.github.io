<pre style="text-align:center;">
I will meet you again
Where? How? I don't know
Perhaps as a figure 
Of your imagination
I will appear on your canvas
Or perhaps on your canvas
Appearing as a mysterious line 
Quietly
I will keep staring at you.

- Amrita Pritam
